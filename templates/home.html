WHO WE ARE

Welcome to our Beauty Salon! My name is Puspa Dahal, and I am a licensed esthetician in the state of New Hampshire since 2018, with over 20 years of experience in the beauty and skin industry. Originally from Kathmandu, Nepal, I bring a unique blend of cultural heritage and professional expertise to every service we provide.

At our salon, we specialize in eyebrow threading, waxing, and tinting. Our goal is to offer precise, personalized care that enhances your natural beauty and leaves you feeling your best. Whether you're here for a quick touch-up or a complete beauty treatment, we strive to create a relaxing and welcoming atmosphere.
